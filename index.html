The Storyteller

Oluwatobi Yeye-Irojah

"Normal is Dead" isn't just a slogan; it's the lens through which I see the world.

For over a decade, I have dedicated my life to the art of visual storytelling. As the Creative Director of Phursuns World, I lead a team of visionaries to bridge the gap between imagination and reality.

My journey is rooted in documentary photography—capturing the raw, unscripted moments that define our shared human experience. Whether behind the camera or mentoring the next generation, my goal remains the same: to disrupt the average and create the extraordinary.
